Gun Violence Prevention is Key in Todays Society

Over the last years gun violence has been increasing and this year it is peaking in Philadelphia. As of July 20th, 1,289 people have been shot and 260 of them have been shot and killed.

This year 120 children have been shot and 21 children were shot and killed. That is an increase of 250% of children killed compared to last year in just the first 6 months.
